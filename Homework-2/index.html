Bunny Fun Facts

Bunnies can live up to 12 years, This also applies to the Flemish Giants, Oddly enough baby bunnies are called kittens, They can turn their ears 180 degrees and have nearly 360 degree vision. Carrots are actually very bad for them and should only be given as treats (it's basically candy to them), And finally they are just critters, just little silly creatures.
